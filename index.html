About Me:

I'm Tobin Cavanaugh. I'm very very passionate about low level programming, and I aim to make a measurable impact on the field of computing. I'm generally unimpressed with the quality of software and the direction we are heading in, and I think we should look to the greats of the previous generations of programmers, those like Dennis Ritchie, (C, Unix), Brian Kernighan (AWK, Unix), and Donald Knuth (TeX, MMIX), to name a few.
